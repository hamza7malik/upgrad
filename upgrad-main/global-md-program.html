Begin your Medical Journey with our 6-Year Global MD Course

About Global MD Program

This 6 year program is specifically designed for high school graduates aspiring to obtain an MD Degree. Program begins with 2 years of premedical courses. Upon successful completion of the premedical phase, students seamlessly transition to the Basic Medical Science (pre-clinical) portion of the degree program.

Our University of South Carolina Modeled program and curriculum prepares students to become competent and well-rounded physicians in the United States, Canada and around the world. The program structure equips learners with the necessary skills and knowledge to successfully pass USMLE® Step 1 and Step 2 prior to residency.

From the very beginning of the program, students benefit from a minimum of 3 hours per week dedicated to clinical observations and rotations, providing invaluable exposure to real-world clinical settings and patient care. With our focus on small class sizes, exceptional faculty, and comprehensive USMLE preparation, we are fully committed to supporting your journey towards postgraduate or residency training programs.
