About upGrad Institute of Medical Sciences

upGrad Institute of Medical Sciences is a premier private medical school situated in the beautiful city of Port Vila, capital of Vanuatu.

Our mission is to offer students affordable and high-quality medical education. We provide comprehensive degree programs including

  • Doctor of Medicine (MD)
  • Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS)
At UIMS, we blend technological advancements with a standardized curriculum, aiming to revolutionize medical education and make it accessible, effective, and impactful.

Hear from our Dean

I take this opportunity to welcome you to the upGrad Institute of Medical Sciences, Port Vila, Vanuatu. 

Located in the picturesque tourist paradise of Port Vila, UIMS aims to train medical students from all over the world, especially from Asia, the Asia-Pacific region and the Australian continent. In the long run, this medical school will surely augment the healthcare facilities available in this region, so also in retaining medical talent here. 

With the backdrop of our Vision and Mission Statement, UIMS has the best and the most experienced international medical teachers as a part of our full-time faculty, and also one of the most reputed clinicians from all over the world as our Adjunct / Part-time faculty. This ensures optimal delivery of a carefully drafted medical curriculum approved by the United States Medical Licensing Examination (USMLE), USA, and the National Medical Commission (NMC – the erstwhile MCI), India. We will have an optimal mix of the traditional didactic system of medical education and the Problem Based Learning methodology, which eliminates the monotony of lecture delivery while ensuring an active involvement of the students in the learning process. The assessment process includes both summative and formative assessments; self-assessment by students is also incorporated.

About Port Vila

Our 6-year program is for recent Port Vila is the capital of Vanuatu, a commonwealth nation in the South Pacific Ocean in proximity to Australia and New Zealand. Port Vila is a beautiful city with a relaxed atmosphere. The city is surrounded by lush greenery, white-sand beaches and is the center of government, business, and tourism. UIMS is proud to be part of this beautiful and vibrant city with something to offer everyone.
