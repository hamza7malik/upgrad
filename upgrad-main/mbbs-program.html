Begin Your Medical Journey With our 5.5-Year MBBS Course

About MBBS Program

Our MBBS curriculum aims to produce doctors who are forward-thinking, well-informed and capable of delivering effective, humane and ethical medical care. We also prepare you to engage in lifelong learning and to have the skills necessary for postgraduate training.

Tailored specifically for high school graduates from the Indian subcontinent, our 5.5-year program combines 4.5 years of on-campus learning with a year-long internship at our academically integrated Vila Central Hospital in Port Vila. We adhere to the esteemed AIIMS curriculum to ensure the highest quality education for our students. Right from the outset, our program emphasises clinical observations and rotations, allowing for a minimum of 3 hours per week dedicated to hands-on experience in real-world clinical settings and patient care.

With our emphasis on small class sizes, exceptional faculty, and comprehensive preparation for the NEXT exam, we are fully committed to supporting your journey towards a successful career in postgraduate or practising medicine.

Admission Process

UIMS welcomes students of all diversity, irrespective of their race, age, gender, religion, or nationality. The registration window opens twice a year. The admission process could take six to eight weeks, depending on the aspirant's response time in the application and selection process.
